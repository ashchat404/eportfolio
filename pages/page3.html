My personal project - VEGGPS

About Veggps

Veggps is an online platform where you can find restaurants near you which serves a particular "vegerarian" dish you are looking for

Functionalities

✔ You can search for a specific dish

✔ You can veiw all vegetarian / vegetarian serving restaurants

✔ You can also submit restaurants details and vegetarian dishes served in it

✔ Customer review on each dish - Comming Soon

✔ Directions to selected restaurants - Comming Soon

How it works?

Well its pretty straignforward. You type a dish name, for example: burger, and we will provide all restaurants which serves "vegeterian" burgers.
