My personal project - VEGGPS

About

Veggps is an online platform where you can find restaurants near you which serves a particular "vegetarian" dish you are looking for

You can

✔ search for a specific dish

✔ veiw all vegetarian / vegetarian serving restaurants

✔ also submit restaurant details and vegetarian dishes served in it

✔ review on each dish - Coming Soon

✔ get directions to selected restaurants - Coming Soon

How it works?

Follow simple steps - You type a dish name, for example: burger, and it will provide all restaurants which serves "vegeterian" burgers near your location. Also you can add restaurants you know by simply clicking "add veggie food" and filling restaurant name and dish name
